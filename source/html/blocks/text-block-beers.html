LOFOTPILS BEERS

Lofoten's cliff befested islands, surrounded by sea, has a nature that is so unique, pure and full of vitality. The pure nature and its waters is what Lofotpils is all about. We aim to create a beer that represents the best of Lofoten, by adding the clean waters of Lofoten and other fresh ingredients in our beer.
Hence our motto: TASTE OF PURE NATURE

Lofoten consists of many small islands, and has a harsh environment, being so exposed to the sea and wind.
Taste of pure nature.

Lofotpils uses Lofoten as inspiration for all beers brewed.
